The Band's History

Imagine Dragons is an American pop rock band from Las Vegas, Nevada, consisting of lead singer Dan Reynolds, guitarist Wayne Sermon, bassist Ben McKee, and drummer Daniel Platzman. The band gained exposure with the release of their single "It's Time", followed by their debut studio album *Night Visions* (2012), which resulted in the chart-topping singles "Radioactive" and "Demons".

Their second studio album, *Smoke + Mirrors* (2015), reached number one on the US *Billboard* 200. The band has become one of the most commercially successful rock bands of the 2010s. They hold the record for the most weeks at No. 1 on the Billboard Hot Rock Songs chart for their hits "Believer", "Thunder", and "Radioactive".
